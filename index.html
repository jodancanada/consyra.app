About Consyra

Built from the
Jobsite Up

Consyra is an in-house technology platform designed to support intelligent project, warranty, and property management.

Born from 12+ years of field experience managing construction projects across Alberta, Consyra addresses the real gaps in how builders, owners, and property managers track issues, manage warranties, and communicate across the lifecycle of a property.

Built for all residential & commercial projects, and can be tailored to meet individual project. Consyra is built in mind to manage CMHC-backed and multi-family properties, the platform brings transparency, accountability, and data-driven oversight to every stage — from construction through long-term asset management.

Platform Capabilities

Smarter Property Intelligence

One platform to manage the full lifecycle of multi-family properties from construction handover to long-term asset management.

Warranty Management

Track warranty periods, log deficiencies, and manage resolution workflows with full audit trails and photo documentation.

Tenant Reporting

Give tenants a simple way to report issues. Route, prioritize, and resolve with clear accountability at every step.

Inspection Workflows

Standardized inspection checklists with photo capture, timestamping, and automated deficiency flagging.

Analytics & Reporting

Data-driven dashboards showing resolution times, deficiency trends, cost tracking, and portfolio-level performance.

Document Hub

Centralized storage for drawings, permits, warranties, and correspondence — searchable and organized by property.

Multi-Stakeholder Access

Role-based access for owners, builders, property managers, and tenants — everyone sees what they need, nothing more.

Our Story

Built by JoDan Group

Consyra is developed by JoDan Group, a construction project management firm with over 12 years of field experience across Alberta.

After managing hundreds of residential units and dozens of commercial sites, we saw firsthand how fragmented the post-construction process is — scattered warranty claims, lost documentation, and disconnected communication between builders, managers, and tenants.

Consyra is our answer: a purpose-built platform that combines deep construction expertise with smart technology to close those gaps.
